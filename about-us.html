Our Story

The Secret Lays in the Experience. Wanting to share the Lebanese culture with everyone who loves new experiences, Al Meshwar shall be your map to the heart of Lebanon. To spread the Lebanese culture, we decided to bring the whole package to Dubai. From the live bread baking and the traditional Lebanese interior, Al Meshwar is much more than a restaurant; it’s a cultural journey with flavorful oriental food.

About us

Home of the finest traditional Lebanese cuisine, Al Meshwar offers you a beautiful, modern and urban Lebanese Experience. Nestled in Dubai - United Arab Emirates, Al Meshwar holds “the Must Se” list of Lebanon, with an interior designed to take you to Lebanon’s famous historic spots. We make sure to bring the traditional Lebanese flavor to your table, for you and your family, to enjoy a perfect experience à la Libanaise. Al Meshwar not only offers the finest traditional Lebanese cuisine as you know it, but it also serves specialty platters that you’re not likely to find easily elsewhere. Lebanese generosity and hospitality is definitely something to try.

The concept

What makes Al Meshwar different is the experience as a whole. It’s not only about the delicious Mediterranean dishes, but it’s also about serving and sharing the Lebanese traditional spirit. So, whether you’re looking to have a full dining experience or you want to come in for a quick bite or you simply want to enjoy our food in the comfort of your home or office, Al Meshwar is the place for you!
